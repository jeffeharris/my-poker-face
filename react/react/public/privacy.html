Privacy Policy

Introduction

My Poker Face ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by Jeff Harris. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our poker game application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Your name and email address when you sign in with Google
  • Game Data: Your gameplay actions, decisions, and game history

How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication: To identify you and maintain your account
  • Game Functionality: To save your games and track your progress
  • Analysis and Improvement: To analyze gameplay patterns and improve the game experience

Third-Party Services

We use AI services (such as OpenAI and Anthropic) to power our AI poker players. When you play, game context may be sent to these services to generate AI responses. These services have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We retain your account information and game data for as long as your account is active.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
